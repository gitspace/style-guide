# Dealing with Public Criticism

As a genera rule, try not to read the comments on your article. The positive comments dont make up for the awful ones. But if you do decide to dip into the hellmouth here are some forulated replied to keep you sane. Originally by [@pourmecoffee](http://pourmecoffee.smallpict.com/?story=finePrint) with minor adjustments.
